Nutella Mug Cake


INGREDIENTS
• ¼ cup all purpose Flour
• 2 Tbsp Cocoa powder
• ¼ tsp Baking Powder • 2 Tbsp Castor Sugar
• 2 tsp Vegetable Oil
• ¼ cup milk
• 2 Tbsp Nutella


Instructions
1) Mix all the dry ingredients- flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and caster sugar in your cup.
2) Once your ingredients are mixed well, add the vegetable oil and the milk.
3) Give the batter a good mix.
4) Add the nutella and mix again.
5) Microwave for 70 seconds.
