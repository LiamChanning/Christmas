Hot chocolate


Ingredients
1. 4 cups milk any type will work
2. 3 tablespoons cacao powder
3. 4 ounces semi-sweet chocolate finely chopped (or approx 2/3 cup chocolate chips)
4. Optional: 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract


Instructions
1. In a saucepan over medium heat, bring the milk, cacao powder, and maple syrup to a simmer. Stir until there's no clumps.
2. Add the finely chopped chocolate and whisk for about 3-5 minutes, or until the hot chocolate is smooth.
3. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs, and if you'd like, top with whipped cream, coconut whipped cream or homemade
    marshmallows and a sprinkle of cacao powder.
